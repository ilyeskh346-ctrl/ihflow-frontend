1. Introduction
Welcome to IH Flow ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our platform at ihflow.com.
2. Information We Collect
We collect the following types of information:
Account Information: Your name, email address, and password when you create an account.
Instagram Account Data: When you connect your Instagram account, we access your Instagram Business account ID, username, and associated Facebook Page information via the Meta API.
Message Data: Customer messages and conversations processed through your connected Instagram account in order to power your automated responses.
Product Data: Product names, images, prices, sizes, colors, and quantities that you upload to our platform.
Order Data: Order details including customer names, phone numbers, addresses, and purchase information generated through your bots.
Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our platform, including pages visited, features used, and time spent.

3. How We Use Your Information
We use the information we collect to:
Provide, operate, and maintain our platform and services.
Power your automated Instagram DM bots and customer response features.
Process and display orders generated through your bots.
Send you service-related communications such as confirmation emails and support responses.
Improve and personalize your experience on IH Flow.
Comply with our obligations under applicable law.

4. Instagram & Meta Data
IH Flow integrates with Meta's Instagram API. By connecting your Instagram account, you authorize us to access and use your Instagram data as described in this policy and as permitted by Meta's Platform Terms. We only request permissions necessary to provide the service. We do not sell your Instagram data to third parties.
The permissions we use include:
instagram_business_basic — to read your Instagram Business profile.
instagram_business_manage_messages — to send and receive DMs on your behalf.
instagram_manage_messages — to manage your Instagram messages.
pages_manage_metadata — to access metadata about your connected Facebook Page.

5. Data Sharing
We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:
Service Providers: Third-party companies that help us operate our platform (e.g., Supabase for database hosting, n8n for automation workflows). These providers are bound by confidentiality obligations.
Meta / Instagram: As required to provide the Instagram automation features.
Legal Authorities: If required by law or to protect the rights and safety of IH Flow, our users, or the public.

6. Data Retention
We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with our services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us or using the account deletion feature in your settings.
7. Data Security
We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
8. Your Rights
Depending on your location, you may have the right to:
Access the personal data we hold about you.
Request correction of inaccurate data.
Request deletion of your data.
Withdraw consent at any time.
Disconnect your Instagram account from our platform at any time.
To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at support@ihflow.com.
9. Children's Privacy
Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
10. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on our website and updating the effective date. Your continued use of our services after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
